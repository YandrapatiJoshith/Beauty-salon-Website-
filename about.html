About

It's All About My Story

I Have Been A Passionate Make Up Artists.

Ever since I was a little girl, I’ve been fascinated by beauty—not just makeup or hairstyles, but the way a woman carries herself after she feels confident. I grew up in a small town, in a tiny two-room house that always smelled like rose water and henna. My mother ran a home-based mehndi and threading service. I’d sit beside her, watching her gently hold a client’s hand, draw delicate patterns, and talk about life, love, and everything in between.

Today, my salon is more than a business. It’s a community. I've trained three girls from my area who now run their own services. I’ve seen teenage girls blossom into confident women. And every time I hand a client a mirror and see that glow on her face, I remember why I started. Because beauty isn’t about hiding flaws. It’s about revealing strength. And I’ll never stop chasing that magic.

Christina Kollar
